- **User problems (interviews):** **Corporate** **work** **paradox**—**pays** **the** **most** but **feels** **the** **least** **inspiring**; **most** **editing** **time** **goes** **to** **prep** **before** the **“** **fun** **”** **part**; **creatives** are **risk‑averse** **to** **changing** **workflow** and **uneasy** **handing** **control** **to** **other** **editors** **or** **AI**.

### Curio

- **Role:** **End‑to‑end** **designer** **&** **researcher**. **Conceptual** **portfolio** **project** (**not** **Spotify** **employment** **or** **briefed** **by** **Spotify**; **uses** **public** **growth** **context**—**Q2** **goal** **to** **add** **~14M** **monthly** **active** **users**—as **the** **design** **prompt**).

- **Survey quote (verbatim):** “**Everything** **is** **already** **set** **up** **in** **Spotify** **&** **the** **recommendations** **are** **dialed** **in.**”

- **Interview quote (verbatim):** “**No,** **I** **am** **so** **ingrained.** **Spotify** **has** **so** **much** **of** **my** **data** **in** **terms** **of** **what** **music** **I** **listen** **to.**”  
- **Pivot —** **“** **from** **conversion** **to** **connection** **”:** **Stop** **centering** **switchers**; **prioritize** **new** **users** **building** **connection** **and** **library** **velocity** **for** **retention**.  
- **Two prototypes —** **Chat** **vs** **Curate:** **Chat** **=** **conversational** **AI**, **playlist** **via** **natural** **dialogue**. **Curate** **=** **selection** **flow** (**artists**, **moods**, **genres**).  
- **Usability tests —** **five** **participants:** **Each** **completed** **playlist** **creation** **with** **Chat** **and** **Curate**; **evaluated** **error** **rate**, **time** **on** **task**, **task** **completion**, **intent** **to** **reuse**.  
- **Chat** **vs** **Curate** **(study):** **Avg** **errors** **per** **user** **—** **Chat** **~0.6** **vs** **Curate** **~9.6**. **Avg** **time** **on** **task** **—** **Chat** **~33s** **vs** **Curate** **~125s**. **Completion** **—** **Chat** **100%** **vs** **Curate** **80%**. **All** **five** **said** **they’d** **use** **Curio** **repeatedly** **and** **preferred** **Curio** **to** **current** **playlist** **creation** **(5/5)**; **4/5** **(80%)** **preferred** **Chat** **over** **Curate**.  
- **Usability quote (verbatim):** “**I'd** **use** **this** **[chat]** **all** **the** **time,** **when** **I** **need** **a** **new** **gym** **playlist** **or** **getting** **ready** **with** **the** **girls**”  
- **Iterations —** **Chat:** **Expansion** **icon**, **inline** **customization**, **scroll** **gradient**, **clearer** **CTA**. **Curate:** **Progress** **bar**, **feedback** **toast**, **confirmation** **step** **to** **refine** **selections** **and** **extend** **customization**.  
- **Product decisions:** **Prioritized** **Chat**, **shelved** **Curate**. **Onboarding** **placement** **—** **Curio** **Chat** **before** **home** **to** **speed** **first** **library** **milestones** **and** **early** **emotional** **investment**.

- **Scaling exploration (conceptual):** **Mapped** **in‑app** **access** **points** **for** **repeat** **use**; **considered** **free** **vs** **premium** **—** **weekly** **usage** **caps** **vs** **ad‑supported** **wait** **during** **“** **generating** **playlist** **”**.
